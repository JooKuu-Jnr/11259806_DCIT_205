FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

We offer Bachelor of Science (B.S.) programs in Computer Science, as well as specialized tracks in areas such as Data Science and Cybersecurity.

To apply, visit our university's admissions website. Make sure to select "Computer Science" as your intended major on your application.

Our faculty members are engaged in cutting-edge research in areas like artificial intelligence, machine learning, cybersecurity, robotics, and computer vision. You can explore their research interests on our website.

Yes, we actively support internship placements for our students. Our career services team works closely with industry partners to help students secure valuable internships.

You can reach us by email at csdepartment@ug.edu.gh

We provide a range of resources, including dedicated computer labs, access to cutting-edge software, tutoring services, and a well-stocked library with computer science reference materials. Additionally, our faculty hold regular office hours for one-on-one assistance with coursework and projects.
